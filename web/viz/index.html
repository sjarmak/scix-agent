SciX Visualization Demos

Four lenses on the 32.4-million-paper corpus.

V2

Temporal Community Flow

Sankey diagram of how papers move between semantic communities across decades. Hover a node to isolate its incident flows.

V3

UMAP Embedding Browser

2-D projection of 100,000 INDUS-embedded papers, coloured by coarse community. Pan, zoom, and click a legend swatch to isolate a community.

V4

Live Agent-Trace Overlay

Type a query to run real hybrid search over 32M papers. Each tool call flashes the touched papers on the UMAP and narrates in the panel in real time over SSE.

V5

Research Topology

20×20 citation heatmap derived from 297.9M citation edges. Reveals which communities cite which — three modes: raw counts, row-normalized, and citation lift.

V6

Citation Ego Network

Force-directed graph of a paper's citation neighborhood — up to 100 refs + 100 cites + 200 2-hop nodes, coloured by community. Drag nodes to rearrange; click a neighbor to re-center.

Corpus: 32.4M papers · 32.4M INDUS embeddings · 100K UMAP points · 20 coarse communities · decades 1800–2020.
